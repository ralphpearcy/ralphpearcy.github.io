Evanescence

The Best Rock Band

Evanescence is an American rock band founded in Little Rock, Arkansas, in 1995 by singer and pianist Amy Lee and guitarist Ben Moody.

After recording independent albums, the band released their first full-length album, Fallen, on Wind-up Records in 2003.
